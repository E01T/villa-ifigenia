Our story

A Modern stone-built villa with amazing sea view and natural surroundings

Villa Ifigenia is a two-level stone building which has been recently constructed. The building is unique because the traditional elements are combined with a modern touch so that the visitors will feel comfortable during their stay. Located in a quiet and isolated area, just 7km away from the lively city of Rethymno. It is well laid out in 80sq metres with an open plan kitchen, dining area and a living room downstairs and two bedrooms upstairs. The swimming pool is also for private use.

Villa Ifigenia is only 15 minutes drive from the centre of the city and 15 minutes drive from the nearest beach. The villa can accommodate up to 4 people.
